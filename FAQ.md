# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### Q: Are these workouts free?
**A:** Yes, free to use, modify, and share. MIT License.

### Q: Do I need a subscription to use these?
**A:** No subscription needed. However:
- TrainingPeaks (free) account for import (recommended)
- Or use directly in Zwift (free to import workouts)
- Smart trainer or power meter helpful but not required

### Q: Can I use these without power?
**A:** You can follow them by RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) or heart rate, but power-based training is recommended for best results.

## Technical Questions

### Q: What file format are these workouts?
**A:** `.zwo` (Zwift Workout XML) - an open, text-based format that works across platforms.

### Q: My platform doesn't accept .zwo files. What do I do?
**A:** Use a converter:
- [What's on Zwift Workout Converter](https://whatsonzwift.com/convert/workouts) (.zwo → .fit, .erg, .mrc)
- Most platforms accept at least one of these formats

### Q: How do I know my FTP?
**A:** Options:
1. Do an FTP test (20min all-out, take 95%)
2. Use Wahoo Half Monty (ramp test)
3. Use Zwift's FTP test
4. Estimate: 75% of your 5min max power

### Q: The workout is too hard/easy. What do I do?
**A:** 
- **Too hard:** Reduce intensity to 95% or 90% in your app
- **Too easy:** Time to retest your FTP! It's probably higher now
- Most apps let you adjust workout intensity on the fly

### Q: Can I modify these workouts?
**A:** Yes! They're `.xml` files - open in any text editor and adjust:
- Duration
- Power targets
- Number of intervals
- Recovery time

## Platform-Specific Questions

### Q: TrainingPeaks won't import my file
**A:** Check:
1. File has `.zwo` extension (not `.zwo.txt`)
2. Using "Workout Import" not "Upload Activity"
3. File isn't corrupted (try re-downloading)
4. TrainingPeaks sometimes takes a few minutes to process

### Q: Wahoo app doesn't show my workout
**A:** 
1. Make sure TrainingPeaks is connected in Wahoo app (Settings → Authorized Apps)
2. Workout must be on "today" or "tomorrow" in TrainingPeaks calendar
3. Wait 5-10 minutes for sync
4. Force close and reopen both apps

### Q: Can I use these with Zwift?
**A:** Yes! Two ways:
1. Import directly to Zwift (Workouts folder on your computer)
2. Or use TrainingPeaks integration with Zwift

### Q: Do these work with Peloton/other platforms?
**A:** If the platform accepts:
- `.zwo` files → Yes, directly
- `.fit`, `.erg`, `.mrc` → Convert first
- Nothing → Sorry, incompatible

## Workout-Specific Questions

### Q: Which collection should I start with?
**A:** 
- **Beginner:** Classic Training Zones
- **Experienced:** Either, or both!
- **Time-crunched:** Classic (simpler structure)
- **Performance-focused:** 4DP-Style

### Q: How many workouts per week?
**A:** Depends on your fitness:
- **Beginner:** 2-3x/week
- **Intermediate:** 3-4x/week
- **Advanced:** 4-5x/week
- Always include 2+ rest days

### Q: Can I do these workouts back-to-back?
**A:** 
- **Same day:** Generally no (unless one is recovery)
- **Consecutive days:** Mix hard and easy
- **Example:** Hard Monday → Recovery/Off Tuesday → Hard Wednesday

### Q: What's TSS and why does it matter?
**A:** Training Stress Score - indicates workout intensity:
- <100: Light, recover in 1 day
- 100-200: Moderate, recover in 1-2 days
- 200+: Hard, need 2+ days recovery

### Q: I don't have 4DP values, can I still use 4DP workouts?
**A:** Yes! Estimated 4DP from FTP:
- **MAP:** 110-120% of FTP
- **AC:** 140-150% of FTP
- **NM:** 2.5-3.5x FTP (max sprint)

## Safety & Training Questions

### Q: I'm new to structured training. Is this safe?
**A:** 
- Start with Recovery/Endurance workouts
- Build up gradually
- Listen to your body
- **If unsure, consult a physician first**

### Q: The workout hurt. Is that normal?
**A:** Depends:
- **Muscle burn:** Normal during hard intervals
- **Joint pain:** Stop immediately
- **Chest pain / dizziness:** Stop, seek medical help
- **Extreme fatigue:** Take extra rest days

### Q: How long until I see results?
**A:** Typical timeline:
- **2-3 weeks:** Feel stronger
- **4-6 weeks:** Measurable FTP gains
- **8-12 weeks:** Significant improvement
- Consistency matters more than intensity!
